# LeResearch.org

Public-benefit research nonprofit (501(c)(3) in formation). Sibling to
[LeDesign.ai](https://ledesign.ai), the commercial LLC.

LeResearch is a small team doing cross-disciplinary research across
any substrate where the LeDesign silo-collapse thesis applies:
environmental systems, household food + nutrition, plural frontends
for expert knowledge, epistemic ecology under AI-mediated knowledge,
and cross-substrate methodology transfer.

> *A small contribution to the silos's fall.*
